HR Analysis

Requirments : The HR wants to analyze attendance data for work from home and work from office to understand preferences and percentages of people.
Key Insight : Attendance fluctuated over three months, peaking in April and declining in May (91.8% overall), with notable WFH increases on Fridays, higher Sick Leave on Mondays, and a 9.2% WFH rise between June 9 and June 17, 2022, while Sick Leave spiked unexpectedly on June 13, 2022 (3.80%).
Suggestions : Considering these trends, transitioning to a hybrid model—incorporating three days in-office and two days WFH per week—could optimize resources, potentially reducing rental expenses if additional space is required.

Hospitality Analysis

Problem Statement Due to strategic moves from other competitors and ineffective decision-making in management, Atliq Grands is losing its market share and revenue in the luxury/business hotels category.
Key Insight : Atliq Grands saw revenue growth and improved customer satisfaction; weekends had higher occupancy rates; other travel platforms accounted for 40% of bookings, with luxury rooms in Mumbai driving revenue.
Suggestions : Atliq Grands should leverage dynamic pricing for peak days and weekends, implement targeted marketing for offline platforms, address critical areas in customer reviews, and increase direct bookings to reduce reliance on online platforms.
